Kid Radd Summarized

Kid Radd is the story of a small group of sprites who travel the web, searching ROMs to find new allies in their war against the Moderators.

That's the story as it is now. If you want to know how it got to this scenario, there's the story guide at left. If you want to know more about a particular sprite, there's the character guide at right.

Of course, the best way to understand what's going on in Radd's world is to read the archives, but these little guides oughta give you what you need to know.
