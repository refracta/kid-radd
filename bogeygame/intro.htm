Bogey Bomber

The object of the game is simple - help Radd blast every Bogey that appears onscreen! To control Radd, pass your mouse pointer over the field of colored boxes. When Radd is lined up with a Bogey, click Radd to make him attack.

But move quickly; if a Bogey stays onscreen for too long, it'll attack and the game will be over. The further in the game you go, the less time you have to bomb the Bogeys.

Survive three levels of Bogey-bombing and you win. Good luck and have fun!
